Our Story

Hidden in the vibrant streets of Ueno, Tokyo, Awakening Cafe is more than just a coffee shop—it's a moment of peace in the bustling city.

👩‍💼 Woman-Owned Business

Artisan Coffee

Single-origin beans from Ethiopia, Honduras, Colombia, and Nicaragua. Expertly roasted and brewed to perfection.

Warm Hospitality

A welcoming space for everyone. LGBTQ+ friendly and woman-owned, we believe in creating a sanctuary for all.

Live Acoustic

Experience live acoustic music in our cozy atmosphere. Perfect for a relaxing Saturday morning with great coffee.
